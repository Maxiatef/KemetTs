Who We Are

Our Story

Kemet Technologies was founded with a clear purpose: to fill a critical gap in the market for a technology partner that is both genuinely capable and genuinely accountable.

Headquartered in Clovis, California, and serving clients across the United States, we have spent more than seven years designing, engineering, and delivering digital solutions for businesses across diverse industries, from e-commerce and logistics to healthcare, real estate, and professional services.

Our name draws inspiration from Kemet, the ancient Egyptian word for the fertile, life-giving land. It reflects our commitment to helping businesses grow and thrive through the power of thoughtful technology.

Today, our team of designers, developers, and strategists operates as a fully remote-capable firm, giving our clients direct access to senior talent without geographic limitations.
