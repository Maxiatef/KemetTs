**OUR STORY**

## Who We Are

Kemet Technologies was founded with a clear purpose: to fill a critical gap in the market for a technology partner that is both genuinely capable and genuinely accountable.

Headquartered in Clovis, California, and serving clients across the United States, we have spent more than seven years designing, engineering, and delivering digital solutions for businesses across diverse industries — from e-commerce and logistics to healthcare, real estate, and professional services.

Our name draws inspiration from Kemet — the ancient Egyptian word for the fertile, life-giving land. It reflects our commitment to helping businesses grow and thrive through the power of thoughtful technology.

Today, our team of designers, developers, and strategists operates as a fully remote-capable firm, giving our clients direct access to senior talent without geographic limitations.

## SEO, GEO & AEO

### Tagline

Be Found — On Search Engines, AI Platforms, and Everywhere Else That Matters

The way people discover businesses is changing fast. Traditional search engine optimization is no longer enough. Kemet Technologies offers an integrated visibility strategy that covers all three dimensions of modern search:

### Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Technical, on-page, and off-page optimization that strengthens your organic rankings across Google, Bing, and beyond.

### Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Positioning your brand to appear prominently in AI-generated search responses — including Google's AI Overviews, Perplexity, and other large language model-powered platforms.

### Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

Structuring your content so it is surfaced as authoritative answers by voice assistants, featured snippets, and AI-powered knowledge panels.

### What's Included

- Keyword strategy and content planning
